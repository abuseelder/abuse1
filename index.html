Understanding Elder Abuse

March 25, 2025

Elder abuse is a pressing issue that can take many forms—from physical and emotional mistreatment to financial exploitation. In this blog post, we explore the various manifestations of elder abuse, equipping you with the knowledge needed to recognize the subtle signs that often go unnoticed.

By understanding these indicators, you can take proactive steps to protect vulnerable seniors in your community. We'll empower you with practical strategies and resources to help prevent elder abuse, ensuring that our elders live with dignity and respect.
